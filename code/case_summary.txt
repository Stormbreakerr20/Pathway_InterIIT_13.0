Summary: Shivani Gupta, a software engineer at Zen Digital Pvt. Ltd., has discovered that her employer monitored her personal phone activities without explicit consent by installing tracking software on her work laptop. The data accessed allegedly includes personal text messages, photos, and social media activity, causing Shivani mental distress. Zen Digital Pvt. Ltd. claims the monitoring is part of their productivity policy outlined in employment terms, while Shivani argues she was not informed about the extent of monitoring on her personal devices. Shivani seeks legal recourse under Indian laws related to the right to privacy, constitutional guarantees, and penal sections on unauthorized access to private data.**
